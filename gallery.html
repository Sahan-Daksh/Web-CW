the first process towards making Eco-friendly reusable products is gathering and collecting plastic waste that is all around our environment.it can range from forests,beaches,populated areas and the biggest of them all..the ocean!.

The next process would be sorting and differentiating.according to the plastic type like PET,HDPE etc.then they go further in the process of cleaning and shredding into flakes or pebbles.

Plastic pollution is a massive environmental crisis. Plastic can take hundreds or even thousands of years to decompose, littering landscapes and oceans. Collecting plastic waste prevents it from entering these ecosystems and causing harm to wildlife and habitats.

Collecting plastic waste allows us to create a more circular economy for plastic. Instead of a linear process where plastic is used once and thrown away, we can collect, reprocess, and reuse plastic waste, minimizing environmental impact.

After we've collected,sorted and successfully recycled plastic, we can create a wide range of everyday goods and acccessories from the recycled plastic!

Some companies are creating masks from recycled plastic bottles using advanced techniques. These masks are designed for breathability and may offer some filtration.

Our phones are practically extensions of ourselves these days, so it's no surprise we want to protect them in style. But why not do it in a way that's kind to the planet? Enter the world of phone cases made from recycled plastic!
